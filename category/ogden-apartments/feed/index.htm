Keystone On Brady Opening August 2017

By Alaina Beger, Marketing & Design

The construction work on Keystone on Brady is entering the home stretch.  Roofing and windows are being installed as we speak, and soon the residential portion of the building will be fully enclosed, followed by dry walling and other interior finishing work.  Interior doors & trim and cabinets are scheduled for delivery on or around May 20th. 

We are still anticipating completion sometime during the month of July, in anticipation of August 1st occupation, and the leasing team is already receiving interest from potential renters.  We are still looking for a tenant (or tenants) for the 1st floor commercial space, so if you know of any businesses that could be interested, please send them our way!

Visit our community website to learn about Keystone on Brady and follow our progress!

Ogden & Company teams are in place to provide expertise and guidance to ensure our partners get the most out of their properties.
